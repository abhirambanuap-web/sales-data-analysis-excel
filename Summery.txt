📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard – Excel Project

This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across different dimensions such as city, region, product category, and time period.

The dashboard provides a clear visual overview of key business metrics including Total Sales, Total Profit, Quantity Sold, and Profit Margin. Interactive slicers for Year, Region, and Month allow users to dynamically filter the data and explore insights.

🔧 Tools Used

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Pivot Charts

Slicers

Data Cleaning

Dashboard Design

📈 Dashboard Features

KPI cards displaying Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Margin %

Sales by City analysis

Sales by Segment distribution

Region-wise Sales %

Category-wise Quantity %

Monthly Sales Trend

Sales by Day of Week

Top 10 Product Sales

💡 Key Insights

Consumer segment contributes the highest sales (~52%)

East and West regions generate the largest share of revenue

Technology category has strong product performance

Sales peak during the last quarter of the year

🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to demonstrate data visualization, business insight generation, and dashboard development skills using Excel for data analysis.
